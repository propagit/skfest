SUBHEADING INTERSTED IN SPONSORING ST KILDA FESTIVAL?

The St Kilda festival is dedicated to promoting both emerging and already established Australian musicians through the staging of a premier event that provides high profile opportunities for musicians to reach new audiences. It's a celebration of Australian music for the Australian community. Taking place against the iconic backdrop of St Kilda Foreshore and Port Phillip Bay, an international tourist destination, the St Kilda Festival stands out from the pack as it the largest patronised, free music festival in the Southern Hemisphere. With no entry fee required, the St Kilda Festival attracts a broad catchment of people, all in support of Australia's new and established artists and talent.
